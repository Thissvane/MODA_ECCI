Español · English

La tabla contiene los términos técnicos en inglés con su traducción al español y su definición basada en el diccionario Cambridge. / The table contains technical terms in English with their Spanish translation and definition based on the Cambridge Dictionary.

English Español Definition Cambridge
Colour theory Teoría del color The study of colour and how it can be combined or used; a set of principles used to understand the relationships between colours.
Hue Matiz / Tono A colour, or a shade that is a mixture of colours; the quality of a colour as determined by its dominant wavelength.
Saturation Saturación The degree of strength or purity of a colour; the amount by which a colour differs from grey.
Semiology Semiología The study of signs and symbols and their use or interpretation; the science that deals with the systems of signs and symbols in communication.
Pantone Pantone A standardised colour matching system widely used in the printing and fashion industries to ensure colour consistency across different materials and media.
Palette Paleta (de colores) The range of colours that an artist typically uses; a set of colours available to an artist or designer for a particular work.
Contrast Contraste A difference between two things, especially when each makes the other more noticeable; in design, the degree of visual difference between elements.
Typography Tipografía The style and appearance of printed text; the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible and visually appealing.
User experience (UX) Experiencia de usuario The experience a person has when using a product or service, especially in terms of how easy or pleasing it is to use.
Interface Interfaz A connection between two pieces of computer software that allows them to work with each other; the way a computer program is presented on screen to make it easy to use.
Responsive design Diseño responsivo A web design approach in which a website or application adjusts its layout and content automatically to provide an optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices.
Visual identity Identidad visual The visible elements of a brand (colours, fonts, logo, design) that together identify and distinguish it in the public's mind.
Chromatic Cromático Relating to colour; involving or producing a range of colours from one extreme to another.
Aesthetic Estético / Estética Relating to beauty and the study of beauty; describing something that is pleasing to the eye because of its appearance.
Hierarchy Jerarquía A system in which people or things are arranged according to their importance; in design, the organisation of visual elements to guide the viewer's attention.
Accessibility Accesibilidad The quality of being able to be reached or used easily; in web design, the practice of making websites usable by as many people as possible, including those with disabilities.
Brand Marca A type of product made by a particular company; the image, name, and associations that a company or product is known by.
Creativity Creatividad The ability to produce or use original and unusual ideas; the quality of being able to create new and original things.
Innovation Innovación A new idea or method; the introduction of new things or ways of doing something, especially in order to be successful in the market.
Digital product Producto digital Any item or piece of media that is stored digitally and can be sold or distributed online, including software, websites, apps, and digital content.

Source: Definitions adapted from the Cambridge Dictionary and the Cambridge Advanced Learner's Dictionary & Thesaurus. Terms were selected based on their relevance to the Moda ECCI conference on colour theory, visual semiology, and FrontEnd development.
